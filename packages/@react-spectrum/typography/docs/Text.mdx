## Content

The Text is related to typography components and is a children to other
components providing formatting and semantic meaning. It is important to note that
the Text component accepts any renderable node, not just strings.

Used to wrap your text strings and elements with other components. Examples of usage
[Dialog](Dialog.html#example) and [Button](Button.html#content).
